About

Commercial Cashew Management and Transport (CCM&T) is a well-established buyer and processor of cashews grown and processed in Ghana, specifically and primarily in the Brong Ahafo region of Ghana. CCM&T specifically targets the European market and has distributors on the ground to expedite receipt and distribution to your door.
