About Us

What We Do

Coin Capital Markets and Trading (CCM&T) is a well-established broker of commemorative gold and silver coins minted in Hungary and sought after by investors all over the world, especially in Asia. Due to the high demand and low available inventory, we are able to offer our clients top dollar on their investments no matter how long they have been held.

As a Hungarian citizen, you have access to products that are not available to some of these international markets, placing our clients in a highly desirable position for negotiation.

CCM&T is by your side all the way. Once you have contacted us or have been contacted by our knowledgeable call center, we will take the time to understand your requirements, explain the current market and the possibilities from the sale of your investment product and create an agreement that meets your needs.

When the documentation has been completed, we will contact our buyers and do everything we can to complete the sale and see that you receive your funds in a timely manner.
