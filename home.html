About Us

What We Do

Commercial Cashew Management and Transport (CCM&T) is a well-established buyer and processor of cashews grown and processed in Ghana, specifically and primarily in the Brong Ahafo region of Ghana.

Due to the high quality of planting materials available to the farmers, mainly from government and research institutions, Ghana cashew farmers enjoy an exceptionally high yield percentage. This allows for the price to consumer to be more than competitive and lower than most other country providers.

In the last few years, Ghana has boosted production of the cashew by 32%, thus providing the processers more product and yielding high numbers of premium, whole cashew nuts to bundle and ship.

CCM&T passes the savings to the cashew buyers in Europe by going directly to the farmers and processing the cashews in the Brong Ahafo regions of Ghana before packing and shipping to europe.
