About Us

What We Do

Co-op Capital Markets and Trading (CCM&T) is a well-established broker of timeshare rights that act as the platform between the seller and their client. The client purchases and bundles together previously owned or currently owned timeshare rights to create global excursions as a vacation experience for their clients that can range from 4 to 16 weeks and span the globe. These are highly sought after and often times the seller can see a big increase between what they paid and what they can get in return from the sale.

CCM&T is by your side all the way. Once you have contacted us or have been contacted by our knowledgeable call center, we will take the time to understand your requirements, explain the current market and the possibilities from the sale of your investment product and create an agreement that meets your needs. You will have an opportunity to talk to the company that is purchasing your rights via a Zoom meeting.
